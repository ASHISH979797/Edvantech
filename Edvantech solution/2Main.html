Traits of an Innovator

What goes on inside an innovator’s mind, and what are the most important traits of an innovator?

It’s elementary, my dear Dr. Watson! Like the famous fictional detective Sherlock Holmes, an innovator must have an inquisitive mind, sound and logical reasoning, and the ability to make the connections that others cannot.



>>Click the 'O' hotspots to view some of the other important traits of an innovator



An innovator has to experiment and ask questions - what if, why and why not, and then challenge the answers.


An innovator has to closely observe details, particularly the details of process / work.


An innovator has to associate and connect problems, process, and people to create new ideas.
